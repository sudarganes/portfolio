How I work

A few principles that guide how I approach both product design and leadership.

  1. Start with clarity

    Align on the problem, constraints, and what success looks like before jumping into solutions. Clarity up front saves time later and creates shared ownership.

  2. Work in the open

    Share early and often—with teams, partners, and leadership. I create space for feedback, bring people along in the process, and make decisions visible.

  3. Balance craft with outcomes

    High-quality design matters most when it moves the needle for users and the business. I aim for work that is both thoughtful and pragmatic.

  4. Invest in people

    Great products come from healthy teams. I prioritize mentorship, psychological safety, and clear expectations so designers can grow and do their best work.
